Topic

The proposed workshop aims to address the challenges and advances in Explainable AI for Time Series. Time series data is omnipresent, as time is an integral component of all observable phenomena. With the increasing instrumentation of our world, sensors and systems are continuously generating vast quantities of time series data. Hence, time series analysis is integrated in principled decision-making and planning in a wide variety of applications across various industries. This has led to an increasing demand for accurate and interpretable time series models. Explainable AI methods can help users understand the analysis and predictions made by these models and build trust in their predictions. The integration of expert knowledge into learning pipelines from time series plays a major role in this context. This workshop will bring together experts and researchers to discuss recent developments and applications of Explainable AI for Time Series data.
